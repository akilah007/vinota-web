About us

Welcome to Vinota, a leading internet telephony service provider that revolutionizes global communication. Since our inception in 2008, we have been committed to offering affordable international calls and connecting people worldwide. With our user-friendly app available for Android and iPhone, Vinota enables users to make affordable and high-quality calls to any mobile or landline number across the globe. Our unique design and simplicity have earned us a reputation as the preferred choice for many users, who regard Vinota as the best calling experience they've ever had.


Our Journey

Vinota was born out of a vision to provide an alternative to traditional calling cards in the UK. In 2008, we launched our services, aiming to disrupt the market and offer cost-effective solutions for international calling. As mobile VOIP technology advanced, we embraced the opportunity and introduced the Vinota mobile app in 2015. Since then, we have continued to expand our reach and serve customers globally. Vinota is a registered business in Australia and the USA.

Our Mission

At Vinota, our mission is simple yet powerful: to provide seamless global connectivity that brings people closer together. Direct-to-Carrier

With our direct relationships with multiple carriers worldwide, we have in-depth knowledge of their rules and regulations. This enables us to maintain compliance and establish the fastest and most secure connection routes for your calls.

Our groundbreaking Adaptive Routing technology ensures that your calls are delivered through the optimal path, avoiding any potential delays or queues. We constantly monitor carrier traffic to select the fastest and most reliable alternative routes, ensuring uninterrupted connectivity.
